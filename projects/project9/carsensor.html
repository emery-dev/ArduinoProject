Car Sensor

About

This project makes use of the ultrasonic sensor to control a car that you will make and keep it from bumping into obstacles. This project will involve creativity in order to construct the car out of available materials and will also involve controlling DC motors using the Arduino Board.

Information

Difficulty: Medium

Estimated time: 2-3 hours

Story

This Project will involve a lot of creativity as you will be creating a car from cardboard. The design is totally up to you, however, your car will need to be large enough to contain the arduino and most likely two breadboards. You will also be provided with paint and stickers to customize your car as you see fit. We will learn how to control DC motors in this project and how to use that in conjunction with the ultrasonic distance sensor.

Components

The following hardware components were used in this project:

  • 1x Arduino Uno
  • 2x Diodes
  • 2x Transistors
  • 2x 220 ohm resistors
  • 4x Wheels
  • 2x DC Motors
  • Wiring
  • Tape
  • Paint/Paintbrush
  • Stickers

Instructions

Overview:

The most important thing to keep in mind when building the car is that the amount of power that is necessary to run the motor could harm the arduino so we will NOT connect the DC motor directly the Arduino Instead we will use a transistor and a diode to ensure that the arduino and the motor are not harmed.

You will have to connect one of the leads from the motor to the collector pin of the transistor and the other lead of the motor to the diode and 5 volt output from the arduino you will need to do this for both of the motors. It may be helpful to have a breadboard on the top of the car with all of the transistors and diodes that connects to the wheels via a breadboard on the bottom of the car to assure enough ground clearance.

For the distance sensor you will hook it up to the arduino in the exact same way as the distance sensor project, and remember to place it unobstructed on the front of your car to assure that no other piece of your car will be in its way.
